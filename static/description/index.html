Walaa Connector for Odoo

Sync your Odoo products and orders with the Walaa loyalty platform

Features

  • Order Sync — Confirmed Sales Orders and paid POS Orders are sent to Walaa automatically.
  • Product Sync — Push your full product catalog to Walaa in one click, or let Walaa pull it on demand.
  • POS Gifts & Rewards — When a customer is selected in the POS, available Walaa rewards and gifts are fetched automatically and presented to the cashier.
  • Multi-gift support — Cashier can select multiple rewards per order; discounts are applied to the correct order lines in real time.
  • Order Requests — View and apply today's Walaa order requests directly from the POS.
  • Multi-company — Each company has its own Walaa brand token and configuration.

Configuration

Go to Settings → General Settings → Walaa and fill in:

  • Enable Walaa — toggle the integration on/off per company.
  • Walaa Brand Token — your unique brand token from the Walaa dashboard.

Requirements

  • Odoo 18 (self-hosted)
  • Odoo modules: sale, product, base, point_of_sale
  • Walaa API reachable from the Odoo server
